Toyota Yaris 2023 Specifications

The 2023 Toyota Yaris is a compact sedan that combines fuel efficiency, practical performance, and modern design. It comes with a 1.5-liter 4-cylinder engine producing around 105 horsepower, paired with a CVT automatic transmission for smooth and economical driving.

Main Features:

  • Engine: 1.5L, 4-cylinder
  • Power: 105 HP
  • Transmission: CVT automatic
  • Fuel economy: around 20 km/L
  • Infotainment: Touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support
  • Safety systems: Airbags, ABS, EBD, and Stability Control

The car is ideal for daily city driving thanks to its compact size and excellent fuel efficiency.
